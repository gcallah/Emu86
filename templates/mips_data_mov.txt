<h4>
             LW
</h4>

<h5>Syntax</h5>

<ul class="nested">
<li>
            LW reg, reg
</li>
<li>
            LW reg, disp(reg)
</li>
</ul>

<p>
            Copies the value of op2 to the location mentioned in op1.
